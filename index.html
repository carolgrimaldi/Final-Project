About Me

University of the Arts London MA alumnus with academic research and experience in graphic design, fashion, ecommerce and luxury management.

A graduate from the Vogue Certificate course at The Condé Nast College of Fashion & Design , with fluency in English and Portuguese. Previously worked at renowned ecommerce platforms such as Farfetch.com and Shop2gether.com. Several short courses in Photography, UX and Advanced Photoshop. Also, two courses at Central Saint Martins: fashion marketing and graphic design summer abroad.

In 2015, received an award for “Best Ecommerce Design and User Experience” for leading the redesign of the website Shop2gether.com.
